# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 9, 2026

Thank you for using **SPIRAL** ("the Application", "we", "our", or "us").

Your privacy is important to us. SPIRAL has been designed with privacy as a core principle. The application operates locally on your device, and we do not collect, store, or transmit your databases, queries, credentials, or personal files to our servers.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Create

SPIRAL stores information locally on your device to provide its functionality. Depending on how you use the application, this may include:

* Database connection configurations
* User preferences and application settings
* Saved queries
* Comparison configurations
* Workspace layout
* Local cache and temporary files
* Autosaved documents for crash recovery
* User profile information you choose to provide
* Backup and restore files you explicitly create

This information remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

## Database Connections

SPIRAL connects directly from your computer to the databases and services you configure.

Database credentials, connection settings, and database communications are used solely to establish direct connections between your device and your selected database servers.

We do not proxy, inspect, relay, or store your database traffic on our servers.

## Analytics

To help improve SPIRAL, the application can collect anonymous usage analytics through **Google Analytics**.

Analytics may include information such as:

* Application version
* Operating system
* Device type
* Feature usage
* Performance metrics
* Crash and error information
* General usage statistics

Analytics are used exclusively to understand how the application is used and to improve stability, usability, and future development.

### Analytics Opt-Out

You are in control of your data.

You can disable analytics collection at any time from the application's Settings. Once disabled, SPIRAL will stop sending analytics events to Google Analytics.

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do **not** collect or upload:

* Database contents
* SQL queries
* Query results
* Documents
* Files
* Database schemas
* Backup files
* Connection passwords
* API keys
* Authentication tokens
* Personal documents
* Clipboard contents
* Any information stored inside your databases

Your databases remain under your control at all times.

## Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device.

SPIRAL does not operate cloud storage or synchronization services and does not maintain servers that store your workspace or database information.

## Third-Party Services

SPIRAL uses the following third-party service:

### Google Analytics

Google Analytics is used solely for anonymous usage analytics, unless you choose to disable analytics.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Security

SPIRAL is designed so that sensitive information remains on your device whenever possible.

While we take reasonable steps to build secure software, you are responsible for protecting access to your computer, databases, exported files, and backups.

## Children's Privacy

SPIRAL is intended for general audiences and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## International Users

Because SPIRAL operates locally on your device and does not transmit your database information to our servers, your information generally remains under your own control regardless of your location.

If analytics are enabled, anonymous analytics data may be processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the application, legal requirements, or our privacy practices.

The updated version will include a revised "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or SPIRAL's privacy practices, please contact us through the project's official website or GitHub repository.

This version is appropriate for the Apple App Store and accurately reflects a privacy-first architecture where all database operations occur locally, with Google Analytics being the only external service and fully optional.
